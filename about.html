About Wasabi House

Wasabi House is an underground, experimental space created by local students in Rangsit, Thailand.

Founded in 2024, it was built as a platform for diverse forms of expression, including music, performances, and exhibitions. The project has supported both local and international underground scenes, creating a space where different cultures and ideas intersect.

Through continuous experimentation and trial and error, Wasabi House has evolved beyond a venue into a living project shaped by its community. It values raw, intimate experiences and encourages people to connect, explore, and express freely.

As of 2026, the physical space is temporarily closed. We are currently preparing for the next chapter and look forward to welcoming you again.
